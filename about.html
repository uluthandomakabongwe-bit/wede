Brief History

Founded in 2020 by a stay-at-home mum, Sweetheart Factorie was built on a simple mission: take the guesswork out of gifting. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a trusted South African brand for families welcoming newborns and toddlers.

💛

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, budget-friendly gifts for new mothers with a focus on convenience and care. We strive to make new mums feel seen, supported, and appreciated through thoughtfully curated gift sets.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the first choice for thoughtful and accessible gifts for mums and families across South Africa and beyond.
