About Us

About Sweetheart Factorie

Sweetheart Factorie is a gifting brand dedicated to making life easier for busy moms and anyone searching for the perfect gift for newborns and toddlers. We focus on combining quality, convenience, and a personal touch in every gift set.

Our History

Founded in 2020 by a stay-at-home mom, Sweetheart Factorie began with a simple mission: to take the guesswork out of gifting. Understanding the challenges of finding thoughtful and practical gifts, the brand was created to offer carefully curated gift sets that bring joy and ease to both the giver and the receiver.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, budget-friendly gifts for new mothers with a focus on convenience and care. We strive to make new mommies feel seen, supported, and appreciated through our thoughtfully curated gift sets.

Our Vision

To be the first choice for thoughtful and accessible gifts for mommies.

Meet the Team

Meet the Owner

Mbali Masondo, a passionate mom and entrepreneur, founded Sweetheart Factorie with the vision of creating a brand that truly understands the needs of new mothers. With her background in retail and a deep love for family, Mbali has been instrumental in shaping the brand's identity and mission.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated professionals who are passionate about creating meaningful gifts for new mothers. From product development to customer service, each member of our team plays a crucial role in ensuring that every gift set we offer is of the highest quality and thoughtfully curated.
