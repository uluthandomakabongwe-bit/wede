Our Business

What we're all about

Sweetheart Factorie is a South African gifting brand built around families with babies and young children. We started from a simple idea: take the stress out of finding presents that feel thoughtful and are still practical, whether you are shopping for your own child, a niece or nephew, or a friend who has just had a baby.

We bring together nursery essentials, toys, and gear such as cots and prams, with clear pricing in rand and an eye on value. Our focus is on mums and carers who are short on time but still want quality and a bit of heart in what they give.

You can read more about how we began, our mission, and the people behind the brand on the about us page.
