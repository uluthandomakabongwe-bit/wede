This project focuses on designing and developing a website for Sweetheart Factories, a business that specializes in thoughtful and affordable gift sets for newborns and toddlers.

The website aims to improve the company’s online presence, increase customer engagement, and provide customers with a convenient way to browse and purchase products online.
 
 
 # Sweetheart Factorie orgaanisation overview
 On the 30th January 2026, an interview was conducted with the owners of Sweetheart Factories to gather information about the business operations, website requirements, and objectives. This interview provided valuable insights into the business and its goals.
 


# Project Overviews
 
This repository contains the front-end development for the Sweetheart Factorie website, transitioning the brand from social-media-only sales to a dedicated online presence.
• Mission: To provide high-quality, budget-friendly gifts for new mothers with a focus on convenience and care.
• Vision: To be the first choice for thoughtful and accessible gifts for mommies.

 # Key Features & Functionality
• Custom Gift Packs: An interactive catalog allowing customers to build their own gift packs.
• Direct Ordering: Integrated "Add to Cart" and checkout functionality.
• Responsive Design: Fully mobile-friendly UI optimized for busy parents on the go.
• Catalog System: Organized product categories for nursery items, toys, and strollers.

# Design & User Experience
The website follows a "kid-focused yet professional" aesthetic to ensure trust and ease of use.
• Color Palette:
• Background: Beige (#F2EAE0) — Harmonious and neutral.
• Primary: Orchid Pink (#E4A3D4) — Energetic, playful, and friendly.
• Text: Charcoal Black (#263238) — High contrast for optimal readability.
• Typography:
• Headings: Quicksand or Fredoka for a friendly, rounded feel.
• Body: Open Sans, Lato, or Nunito for clean readability.
• Layout: A neat grid layout for product browsing with a "sticky" navigation bar for quick access to the cart and search.

# technincal requirments: 
• HTML5: Semantic structure for SEO and accessibility.
• CSS3: Custom styling featuring Flexbox/Grid for the responsive catalog.
